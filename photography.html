Interactive Media Workshop | Photography & Photo Editing

The Theme of life: The collection of images you are about to see were captured and processed for a Photography and Photo editing project for our Interactive Media Workshop Module.

The Goal: The goal of this project was to demonstrate our ability to compose, take and edit photographs with a theme of Life, including discussion of how the photo relates to the theme, details of the composition of the photo, and technical details - including camera settings and what processing was applied to the image.

In order to achieve these requirements, I strived for a strong composition in all images using a variety of techniques. I also ensured all technical aspects were met while taking and processing the images, paying special attention to the exposure settings while capturing the images and during my processing workflow. I adopted a global to local workflow to my editing process by making changes that apply to the entire image first, then working my way down to changes that apply onto sections of the image. Lastly, I took 16 images and narrowed them down to 5 strong contendors that had a clear connection to the theme assigned to us.

Reflection : I found this project thoroughlly enjoyable. I am a photography enthusiast and it's one of my favourite hobbies to do in my spare time, so I was delighted to see this incorporated into our syllabus this semester. I was ambitious to learn more skills to elevate my own images and undertake new post-proccessing techniques that we were taught in class. Reflecting on my output, I was really satisfied with the output. The photography and photo editing skills I acquired from this module over a very short time has eqquipt me for taking beautiful images in the future, and knowing the essential technical aspects I must consider while the capturing and processing my images.
