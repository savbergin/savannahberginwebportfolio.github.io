Product Proposal Video Demo | IMD Workshop

Our Group Project: As part of our Interaction Design Module, our group designed and developed a physical and digital document management system. The system is based on a digital application to capture, manage and store both physical and digital documents. Following this, we curated a Product Demo Video of the prototype

Software used:We developed the digital prototype in Figma and we used a diverse variety of software to develop the final deliverable, including; Adobe Premier Pro, Canva, Splice, Audacity, and Adobe After Effects with graphics generated using Adobe Photoshop and Adobe Illustrator.

Reflection:It is an understatement to say this project was a significant learning curve for the team. So far, each project focused on an individual type of media - this project encapsulated all these different medias together. Despite the challenge, it was very reawarding to see our final prototype we spent months working on fully developed and presented in our own Product Demo video. Creating this video and seeing our hard work and dedication come to life in the final video made us feel like it was significantly worth all the time that it took! I hope that my dedicated efforts to learn and utilise new software and techniques from this project will stand me as I progress through our Masters Programme.
