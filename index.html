Bio

Hi, I'm Savannah. I am a dynamic, driven opportunist, ample with ambition and innovative ideas. UX Design, Innovation, Business and Technology are my passions, and I am extremely motivated to pursue a career in User Experience Design and develop core technological skills to advance my employability in this industry. Currently, I am completing my Masters in User Interaction and Experience Design at University of Limerick. Upon graduating, I wish to pursue a career in this field where I can apply my passion for designing digital user experiences and gain diverse exposure to technological innovation.

As a digital design advocate and developer, I believe the perfect user interface should look good and work effiently. Being curious and entrepreneurial minded, I enjoy uncovering user problems and in tern, solving them. In short, I aim to create bolder and memorable digital experiences through functional and aesthetic design.

I'm a driven individual and I'm always eager to start a new project. So what are you waiting for? Send me a message and let's start collaborating together!
